about me

Designer and Web Developer from Chicago. He/him has a BFA in Graphic Design from the School of Computing and Digital Media at DePaul University.

For the last several years, I have worked in the design, printing & manufacturing of large-format graphic media at the print studio Colorphonic.

I am available for freelance hire. The following pages are a collection of my work and a sample of the services I offer. You can reach me directly using the contact information below
